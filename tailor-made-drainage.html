Tailor Made Drainage Products

Overview

Industrial-Grade Drainage, Precisely Fabricated

Al Dhabi Steel manufactures a comprehensive range of stainless steel drainage systems engineered for demanding commercial, industrial, and institutional environments. Our product portfolio covers floor drains, linear channel drains, slot drains, shower drains, decorative drain grating, and custom drain covers all fabricated from high-grade AISI 304 and 316 stainless steel.

Every drainage product is manufactured to withstand heavy foot traffic, chemical exposure, and the harsh environmental conditions of the UAE climate. We work closely with MEP consultants, project engineers, and contractors from the early design stage through to on-site installation support. Our drainage solutions meet international hygiene standards and are widely deployed in commercial kitchens, food processing facilities, hotels, hospitals, shopping centres, and industrial plants.
