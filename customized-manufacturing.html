Customized Manufacturing

Overview

Engineered to Your Exact Specifications

Al Dhabi Steel's Customized Manufacturing division specializes in producing bespoke stainless steel and metal components engineered precisely to client requirements. From hygienic food-grade counters and vegetable display trays to heavy-duty wash basins, security counters, and decorative ceiling assemblies, each product is crafted with rigorous quality control at every stage.

Our experienced engineers work directly with architects, contractors, and project managers to translate technical drawings into finished products. With advanced CNC cutting, precision bending, and mirror-polished finishing capabilities, we manufacture to internationally recognized standards including ASTM, EN, and local UAE regulatory requirements. Every product carries our hallmark of durability, functionality, and aesthetic refinement.
