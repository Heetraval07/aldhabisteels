Industrial Kitchen Works

Overview

Heavy-Duty Kitchen Fabrication for Hospitals & Factories

Al Dhabi Steel specialises in the fabrication and installation of heavy-duty kitchen systems designed to withstand the rigorous demands of industrial-scale catering environments. Our industrial kitchen works serve hospitals, food processing facilities, factory canteens, military camps, and other high-volume institutional settings where hygiene, durability, and operational efficiency are non-negotiable. All equipment is fabricated from AISI 304 and 316L stainless steel, meeting stringent food-safety and health authority standards.

From large-capacity exhaust hood canopies and grease management systems to multi-compartment sink banks, heavy-gauge preparation benches, and purpose-built storage solutions, we engineer every component to handle continuous production loads. Our team manages the entire project lifecycle — from initial facility planning and MEP coordination through to fabrication, delivery, and on-site installation — ensuring your industrial kitchen meets regulatory compliance and operates at peak performance from day one.
