Fitout Solutions

Overview

Transforming Spaces with Precision Metalwork

Al Dhabi Steel's Fitout Solutions division provides comprehensive metal fabrication for interior and exterior fitout projects across the commercial, hospitality, retail, and residential sectors. We deliver everything from decorative ceiling systems and wall cladding to precision-engineered structural components, staircases, balustrades, and bespoke furniture frames.

Our design-to-installation approach ensures every element is fabricated to the tightest tolerances and finished to the highest aesthetic standards. Whether you need mirror-polished stainless steel accents for a luxury hotel lobby or powder-coated steel framework for a retail flagship, our team of experienced fabricators and project coordinators deliver on time and to specification — every time.
